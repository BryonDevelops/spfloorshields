ABOUT US

Spectrum Products began operations in 2008 as a supplier of a unique floor covering for gel coat booths in the fiberglass industry. Since then, Spectrum has expanded its product mix to include other innovative floor coverings, surface protection, plastic masking film, and many other consumable products related to the application of paint. Spectrum serves the Marine, Automotive, RV, and Composites industries with bold cost saving solutions to improve labor efficiency as well as improving overall product quality.
